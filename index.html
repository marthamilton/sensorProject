About This Project2

  • Many parents and teaching professionals are not aware of the threat posed by air pollution around school environments and the possible long-term effects. The aim of this project is to utilise computer hardware and data visualisation to provide parents, governors, teachers, students, local MPs and the general public with an up to date and accessible way of understanding the air quality in and around schools.
  • Unfortunately,this project has been majorly affected by COVID-19. During implementation schools have closed and the general public including myself have been told to self isolate. Therefore, the live sensor used in this project has not been placed in a school environment. As a result, the data displayed throughout this project is not linked to a school or in no way accurate for the area. The data displayed will allow you to gain an understanding of the capabilities of this project if all schools agreed to place sensors in their environment.
  • After you have findished exploring the website, if you could give some feedback using "send feeback" in the menu on your thoughts.
